About the Office
Serving Damagatla village under Nandikotkur Mandal, Nandyal District, Andhra Pradesh

🌾 Our Mission

The Swarna Gramam Swarna Ward programme envisions transforming Damagatla into a self-sustaining, prosperous village — ensuring clean water, proper sanitation, good roads, livelihoods, and social security for every family in our community.

🏗️ Village Development

We coordinate with DRDA, Gram Panchayat, and line departments to plan and execute infrastructure works — roads, drains, streetlights, and public amenities — under approved budgets through the Engineering Assistant's supervision.

📋 Welfare & Social Security

Our Welfare Assistant and field staff facilitate pensions, housing, women & child welfare, and health services. We assist residents with documentation, applications, and grievance resolution at the Panchayat Office.

🌿 Health & Community

Our ANM and AHA ensure maternal health, immunization, and health awareness. Village Servants and Assistants connect households to government services and maintain village-level records and attendance.
